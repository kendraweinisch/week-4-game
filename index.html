Crystal Collector Math Game

Instructions

There are four beautiful crystals below, each worth a different amount. Click on each crystal to discover its value. Continue to click on the four crystals until your sum equals the random number in the box on the left. When your total score equals the random number on the left, you win! You lose the game if your total score goes higher than the number on the left.
